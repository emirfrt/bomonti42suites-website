Suites

Our spacious 1+1 and 2+1 suites offer all the comforts of home with modern furnishings, fully equipped kitchens, high‑speed Wi‑Fi and daily housekeeping. Whether you're staying for a short break or a longer trip, you'll appreciate our attention to detail and warm hospitality.

Foca Bomonti

Located on the ground floor of our building, Foca Bomonti is a cosy café and bar that serves specialty coffee, fresh pastries and a curated selection of cocktails and wines. Start your day with a delicious breakfast, grab a mid‑day pick‑me‑up, or unwind with friends in the evening.

Location

Bomonti 42 Suites sits in the vibrant Bomonti neighbourhood, just minutes from Osmanbey metro station and within easy reach of Taksim Square, Nişantaşı shopping district and the historic Old City. The area is filled with art galleries, restaurants, and cultural venues.
